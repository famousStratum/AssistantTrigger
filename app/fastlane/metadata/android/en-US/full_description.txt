AssistantTrigger is an ultra-lightweight utility designed to bridge your physical hardware buttons (like the Samsung Bixby button or other programmable keys) to your system's default voice assistant.

Why use AssistantTrigger?
On many devices, mapping a button directly to an assistant app (like Gemini or Google Assistant) opens the full-screen app interface. AssistantTrigger instead fires the system's "Voice Command" intent, providing a faster, more integrated experience.

Key Features:
- Instant Trigger: Launch the voice assistant overlay immediately.
- Zero Permissions: No internet, no microphone, no storage access needed.
- Minimalist: Uses a "NoDisplay" theme—no intrusive UI or splash screens.
- Clean Recents: Automatically hides itself from your recent apps list.
- Small Footprint: APK size (< 50KB).

How to use (Samsung devices):
1. Go to Settings > Advanced Features > Bixby key.
2. Select "Double press to open Bixby".
3. Enable "Use single press" and select "Open app".
4. Choose "AssistantTrigger" from the list.

Perfect for mapping dedicated hardware buttons to modern AI assistants.
